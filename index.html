What Is SwampHacks?

Unofficially and preemptively dubbed the greatest Hackathon, SwampHacks seeks to inspire all to come together to create. We welcome students, professionals, and hobbyists to attend and innovate!

If you don't have any technical skills, but are bubbling with ideas, scout an attendee to realize your vision. They may not have an idea, yet.

Currently, we're hoping to hold it in Keene-Flint Hall!
